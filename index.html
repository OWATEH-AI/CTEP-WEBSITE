About CTEP

Bridging Art, Technology & Education

The Creative & Technology Exposure Program (CTEP) is a structured educational initiative hosted at ARTGALZIM CENTER in Domboshava, Zimbabwe. It is designed to bridge the gap between traditional classroom learning and real-world creative and digital exposure.

CTEP blends art appreciation, technology demonstrations, ICT skills training, and career guidance into structured day programs for schools. Aligned with ZIMSEC project-based learning objectives, the program prepares students for a rapidly evolving world.

From guided gallery tours to live AI demonstrations and hands-on ICT workshops, CTEP provides students with experiences that inspire confidence, creativity, and practical skills for the future.

i

Please note: All programs and bookings are conducted as day events only. Accommodation is not provided. No overnight stays, residential camps, or boarding services are offered.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are events day-based only? +

Yes. All CTEP programs and events are conducted as day events only. We do not provide accommodation, overnight stays, residential camps, or boarding services of any kind.

Is accommodation provided? +

No. ARTGALZIM CENTER does not offer accommodation. All participants must arrange their own transport to and from the venue. Programs typically run between 2.5 to 6 hours depending on the booking.

Can we host our own event at the venue? +

Yes! Schools can book the venue for their own educational events such as quizzes, debates, workshops, and leadership sessions. You can bring your own facilitators or request ARTGALZIM to provide them.

Can ARTGALZIM provide facilitators? +

Yes. We have experienced facilitators for leadership training, career guidance, digital literacy, and creative workshops. This can be arranged as part of your booking.

How long are sessions? +

The core Student Program runs 2.5 - 3 hours. Seminars and workshops vary from 2 - 5 hours. Full-day events can be arranged with morning and afternoon sessions.

How do we apply? +

Use the application form on this page, or contact us directly via WhatsApp at +263 786 367 366, or call +263 772 479 492. We'll confirm your booking within 24 hours.

What payment methods are accepted? +

We accept Cash, EcoCash, InnBucks, and Bank Transfer. Payment details will be provided upon booking confirmation.
